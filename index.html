4. Investor Move Data (Gold Feature)

Investor holdings shown in the "Billionaire Moves" and related features are derived from publicly available SEC Form 13F filings. Under SEC rules, 13F filings reflect long U.S. equity positions as of the end of a calendar quarter and are filed up to 45 days after that quarter ends. This means the data you see may be up to four and a half months old and does not reflect:

  • Current positions — the investor may have sold, added to, or eliminated any holding since the filing date
  • Short positions, options, derivatives, private investments, or non-U.S. holdings
  • The size of any position relative to the investor's total wealth
  • The reasons behind any purchase or sale

Copying famous investors is not a guaranteed strategy and can produce very different results due to timing, position sizing, tax treatment, and risk capacity differences.
